Tell Me Where To Eat

Tell Me Where To Eat is a fullstack web app that provides a solution to psychological analysis paralysis (not being able to make a decision when there are many options), simplifying dinner decision making with a click of a button.

I'm excited to have leveraged Python's Random Module's Choice method in the backend logic. I grew my knowledge on CSS while creating this app. I really dug into accessibility, and that includes skip navigation, ARIA, large font size, high color contrast, and responsiveness, with a major emphasis on simplification and consistency.

Technologies Used: Python, Flask, Javascript, HTML, CSS, Google Font API, datasets through research and APIs.
